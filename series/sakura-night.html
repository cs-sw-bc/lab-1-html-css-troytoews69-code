Sakura Night

A slice-of-life drama about friendship, magic, and late-night adventures in modern Tokyo.

Synopsis

In modern-day Tokyo, college student Aoi discovers she can see magical spirits, but only after sunset. As she navigates her night-time adventures with new supernatural friends, she must balance her regular student life with her growing role in the city's magical community.

Main Characters

  • Aoi Tanaka - A cheerful college student who loves late-night ramen
  • Kei - A fox spirit who runs a magical convenience store
  • Yuki - Aoi's best friend who slowly discovers the truth
